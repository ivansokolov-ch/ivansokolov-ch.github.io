About

I am a videomaker and photographer based in Zurich, Switzerland. I mainly focus on advertising, music and corporate productions, working both with natural light and in the studio. Following big Italian artists on tour, I have also gained a lot of experience as a concert and event photographer. Cinema is my greatest passion. Hence also the desire to produce and direct short films, as in the case of "Limbo" (2021) and "To a Hard Knot a Sharp Blade" (2022) and several projects still in progress. Thanks to experience with different agencies fields, I am able to both direct a project, as well as playing an individual role within a larger team.
